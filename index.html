About Us

  • PENDO-MEDICARE is a well-established hospital that was founded in the year 1990 in the heart of Muranga County.
  • Over the past decades, we have grown to become a beacon of hope and a trusted healthcare provider, offering top-tier medical services and compassionate care to the local community and beyond. Since our founding, we have remained steadfast in our mission to improve access to healthcare for individuals across all socio-economic backgrounds.
    • Our journey began as a modest healthcare facility, but thanks to the overwhelming support of the community and our unwavering commitment to serve, we have expanded into a fully equipped, modern hospital.
  • This growth has allowed us to extend a wide array of specialized services to address the diverse medical needs of our population.
    • General Medical care
    • Maternity services
    • Childcare services
    • Outpatient services
  • One of the greatest testaments to our impact is the number of lives we’ve touched—thousands of children who are now thriving adults, and seniors who enjoy their golden years in good health, thanks to the early interventions, treatments, and preventive care we have provided.
